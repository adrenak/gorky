# Post template

This is a new post template. Let's have a look at the markdown source, specifically the frontmatter section. 

Notice that it's `published` key is set to `no` so this post won't be visible when you build.
  
`slug`, `title` and `date` are mandatory keys. Date is yyyy-mm-dd
  
`tags` and `description` are optional, but you'd probably want that. Tags allow you to group your posts. And description is a short sentence that's shown below the tiele of this post.

`author` and `keywords` are for SEO purposes, they won't be shown.

`thumbnail` should point to an image anywhere in the repo. I would suggest `content/images`
